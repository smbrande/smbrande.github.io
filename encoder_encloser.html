Absolute Encoder Housing | Fall 2020

I designed absolute encoder housings for the 6Dof robotic arm cycloid transmission joints for my university Mars rover team. The housing contains an absolute encoder PCB that interacts with a magnet mounted on an idler gear that meshes with a motor output gear at the end of the cycloid joint. I was able to overhaul the design this year by adding a securely mounted 3D printed enclosure instead of last year’s L-bracket design mounted by a single shaft pressed into the cycloid gearbox.
